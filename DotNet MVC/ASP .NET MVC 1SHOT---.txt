**#3 ROUTING :**

Routing in ASP .Net MVC is the process of mapping the URLs to their corresponding Controllers and Actions .
-The frontend and the backend, for eg, WE need to map the home Page URL, to the action that the home page should open. SO we need to map the URLS with their appropiricate controller and actions.

-ROUTE TABLE : defines URL pattern and their corresponding handlers, Means it will have the mapping between the URLS and their action or page that should be loaded.
-URL SEGMENT : parts of the URL that determine which controller and action to invoke.
