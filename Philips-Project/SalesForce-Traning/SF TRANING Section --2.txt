ðŸ“ŒRELATIONSHIPs 
-Realationships are same as 1 to 1, many to 1, many to many.
-SF Relationships define how objects are connected with each other. Like foreign key .
MOST USED REALTIONSHIPS : 
	1) LOOKUP REALTIONSHIPs
	2) MASTER REALTIONSHIPs
-SF doesn't support the Master relationships between the standard objects
-SF only allows uh to create the relations between the Custom created objects.

LOOKUP RELATIONSHIPs : it menas its a loosly coupled relationship, it means child records can be exixts without the parent records, and parent records will not delete the child

MASTER RELATIONSHIP : Tightly coupled relationship, The child records depends on the Parent ones. Child Inhirits the security, ownerships and depends on the parent.
-we use this when there is dependency of data, like one object records depends or is created only when the parent record is created , and even if the parent record is deleted then the child record will also get deleted.
-Its kind of foreign key cascading.
-EG: History data, without a user, what will uh do with his user history data, so here Uh will apply master realtionship
