ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library) is a globally recognized framework that provides best practices and guidelines for managing IT services effectively. It helps organizations deliver reliable, efficient, and customer-focused IT services by standardizing processes like incident management, change management, and problem management. ITIL focuses on aligning IT operations with business goals, ensuring quality service delivery and continuous improvement.

ITSM (IT Service Management), on the other hand, is the actual practice of managing and delivering IT services to meet business and user needs. It includes all the processes, tools, and roles involved in designing, delivering, supporting, and improving IT services.
